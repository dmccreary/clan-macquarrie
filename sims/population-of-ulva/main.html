Population of Ulva Island

1000 CE - Present Day

Key Historical Events:

  • • 1780: Peak population of ~725 people
  • • 1794: Last MacQuarrie chief sells Ulva, beginning decline
  • • 1846-1857: Highland Potato Famine causes mass emigration
  • • 19th century: Highland Clearances devastate the community
  • • Present: Fewer than 10 permanent residents remain
